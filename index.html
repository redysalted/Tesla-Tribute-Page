Nikola Tesla

The man who revolutionised electrity.

Nikola Tesla Facts:

  • Nikola Tesla was born on the 10th of July 1856, in the Austrian Empire (Today known as Croatia).
  • In 1884 Nikola moved to the United States and was hired by Thomas Edison.
  • After a few months working with Edison, the two parted ways and Tesla began to create his own inventions.
  • Nikola Tesla went on to create many inventions such as:
    • The AC Eletrical System.
    • The Tesla Coil.
    • The Magnifying Transmitter.
    • The Tesla Turbine.
    • He helped build the Niagara Falls Electric Transformer House.
    • The Induction Motor.
  • Nikola Tesla died in the United States on the 7th on January 1943 (Aged 86).
